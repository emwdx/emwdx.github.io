Embeddings + Genetic Search

Privacy-First Grouper GA

This variant uses local embeddings plus a genetic algorithm to search many candidate classroom groupings locally. Conflict pairs are never sent to a model, but they strongly affect the solver score and remain visible in the manual workspace.

Privacy Boundary

What leaves the browser

Only a stable local student ID and topic text are sent to Gemini embeddings. Names, emails, helpers, contacts, Spanish fluency, and enemies lists stay local. Candidate solutions, conflict scores, and drag/drop edits are all computed in the page.
